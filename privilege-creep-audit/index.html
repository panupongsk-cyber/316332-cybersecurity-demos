💡 Classroom Walkthrough Script & Discussion

This demo demonstrates the importance of Identity Lifecycle reviews and the concept of Least Privilege (Week 2):

  • Semester 1 (Join): Bob is created as a member. Look at the JWT payload—he has standard member roles and permissions.
  • Semester 2 (Promotion): Click "Advance Timeline". Bob is promoted to Club Officer. His role list is expanded with "officer" and "db:write" / "member:export" permissions so he can manage rosters.
  • Semester 3 (Step Down - The Creep): Click "Advance Timeline" to enter Semester 3 without doing anything else. Bob steps down. However, the system's log warns that **Privilege Creep has occurred**—his token still contains "officer" rights because no one ran an access review!
  • Mitigating the Risk: Click **"Reset Simulator"**, advance to Semester 2, and then to Semester 3. While in Semester 3, click **"Run Access Review Audit & Revoke Roles"**. Note that the warning disappears, and Bob's permissions revert to the minimal set needed for a regular member.
